# Instructions

1.  **Prepare the Meat Mixture**
    In a bowl, combine all ingredients except butter.

2.  **Mix**
    Mix the ingredients thoroughly by hand until the spice blend is fully integrated and the mixture is uniform.

3.  **Shape the Logs**
    With wet hands, spread the mixture onto parchment paper and roll it tightly into a log shape.

4.  **Bake**
    Place the rolls in a baking dish and bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 25 minutes.

5.  **Broil and Finish**
    Remove the rolls from the oven and drain any liquids. Unwrap the meat and place it flat on a tray. Brush the top with butter and broil for up to 5 minutes until lightly charred. Slice thinly before serving.
