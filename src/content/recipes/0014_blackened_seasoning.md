ingredients:
    - "1 1/2 tbsp Paprika"
    - "1 tbsp Garlic powder"
    - "1 tbsp Onion powder"
    - "1/2 tbsp Dried thyme"
    - "1 tsp Black pepper powder"
    - "1 tsp Cayenne pepper powder"
    - "1 tsp Dried basil"
    - "1 tsp Dried oregano"
    - "3/4 tsp Salt"
recipeType: "Spices"
mainIngredient: "Vegetarian"
country: "USA"
---

# Instructions

1.  **Combine**
    Measure all the spices and herbs into a small jar or mixing bowl.

2.  **Mix**
    Stir or shake thoroughly until the mixture is uniform and the color is consistent.
