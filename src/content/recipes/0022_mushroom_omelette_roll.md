# Instructions

1.  **Preheat Oven**
    Preheat the oven to 200°C.

2.  **Prepare Batter**
    To prepare the batter, first mix the flour into the milk. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl and add them to the flour and milk mixture. Spice with salt and white pepper.

3.  **Bake Omelette**
    Line a high-edged baking plate (or rimmed baking sheet) with greaseproof paper and pour the omelette batter onto the paper. Bake in the oven until light brown.

4.  **Overturn Omelette**
    Overturn the baked omelette onto another piece of greaseproof paper and carefully peel away the paper from the bottom.

5.  **Cook Mushroom Filling**
    Chop the onions for the filling. Fry the mushrooms in butter or oil. Add the chopped onions and cook until the liquid has nearly evaporated.

6.  **Season Filling**
    Spice with salt and black pepper, and, if you like, add some basil or thyme. Check the taste. *(Note: In case you prefer the filling to be more sauce-like, add a little cream or crème fraîche to the mixture and let it cook for a while).*

7.  **Roll and Top**
    Spread the mushroom mixture evenly over the omelette and roll the sheet up. Carefully move the roll onto a greased ovenproof plate and sprinkle grated cheese on top.

8.  **Final Bake**
    Heat the omelette roll in the 200°C oven until the cheese is melted.
