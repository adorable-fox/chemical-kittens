# Instructions

1.  **Prepare Fenugreek**
    Measure the dried fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi) and crush them firmly between your palms to break them down before adding to the mix.

2.  **Combine**
    Measure all the spices into a small jar or mixing bowl.

3.  **Mix**
    Stir or shake thoroughly until the mixture is uniform in color.
