    - "4 tbsp Oyster sauce"
    - "2 tbsp Light soy sauce"
    - "1 tbsp Dark soy sauce"
    - "1 tbsp Fish sauce"
    - "2 tsp Sugar"
    - "2 tsp Water"

# Instructions

1.  **Combine**
    Measure all the ingredients into a small jar or bowl.

2.  **Mix**
    Stir or shake vigorously until the sugar has completely dissolved and the sauce is uniform.

3.  **Usage**
    Use 1 tbsp of this sauce per one serving of stir-fry (e.g., added towards the end of cooking vegetables or meat).
